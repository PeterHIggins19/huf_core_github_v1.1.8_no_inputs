# Answers — Foundations

1. w_r is total score in regime r; p_r = w_r / Σ w_r is the normalized share over regimes.
2. After dropping items, recompute normalized shares so sums return to 1 over the retained set (while logging what was dropped).
3. It turns long-tail distributions into a reviewable active set, but must remain accountable via EB.
4. To compare items fairly inside the same regime, independent of regime size.
5. Every discard must be logged with reason and mass contribution (EB).
6. Loss becomes invisible and reviewers cannot tell whether outcomes are due to filtering or real structure.
