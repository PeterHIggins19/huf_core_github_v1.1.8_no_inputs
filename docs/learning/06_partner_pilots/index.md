# Partner pilots

What this page is:
A conservative template for applying HUF with external partners without overclaiming.

Why it matters:
Partners don’t want a new theory — they want a safe, auditable workflow.

What you’ll see:
- Scope boundary rules
- A minimal pilot structure
- What artifacts you share and why

## Scope boundary (required)

HUF is an audit layer over *governance of data*, not domain modeling.

Example:
- Environmental governance is the governance layer.
- Ecology is the subject matter.
HUF audits the governance layer; it does not model wetlands.

## Pilot structure (minimal)

1) Agree on regime labels (teams / sites / categories)
2) Run HUF to produce CM/AS/TR/EB
3) Review EB first (discard accounting)
4) Use CM to target questions
5) Keep TR as the audit log
